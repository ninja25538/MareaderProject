It all starts with a letter

SPOILER ALERT!!!!

Jonah and Chip recieve one day that has them racing through time with Jonah's sister, Katherine, to save time. When they first recieve the letters, they start doing reaserch and learn that they a bunch of kids who are adopted also got the same letters, and they all live in the same area in Ohio. During a meeting about how they are adopted, they are led to a cave within a mountain during an adoption confrence with some other kids. There they are kidnapped by Gary and Hodge who tell them they will be sent to the future to be adopted by parents. However JB (Janitor Boy, you'll understand if you read the book) does not agree and instead wants to send them to the past since they are missing kids from history. In the end, JB wins and sends Katherine, Chip, and Jonah back to the past.
